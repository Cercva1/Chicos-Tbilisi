Our Space

Raw concrete,
soft curves,
bold character

Chicos is a study in contrasts — exposed concrete ceilings hover above cream-upholstered banquettes, chrome-legged tables catch the light, and towering tropical plants breathe life into every corner.

  • Signature cream & red seating areas
  • Raw concrete industrial architecture
  • Curated editorial magazine wall
  • Floor-to-ceiling windows with natural light
  • LGBTQ+ friendly & welcoming to all
